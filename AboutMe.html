Waking up everyday is a lucky chance for all of us to be better humans than we were yesterday.

At first, I had no idea what was expected of me in the Digital Arts course, but I grew to understand and engage more with it.

I did not get the chance to enjoy my first year in the university and get my mind at ease that I am not a high school kid anymore. I did not think I was going to be able to manage because I had to go study at home and there were so many distractions and challenges. Gratefully, I passed all my modules, even though they were not the marks I was hoping for, I am thankful that I did not have to repeat any of them.

Now I am a third year Digital Arts student in the Wits School of Arts. I chose to major in Interactive Media and Animation, I have no regrets for the choice I made as I am enjoying the two modules so far, even though I feel like there is going to be a lot of work as time goes, I am ready to do the best I can. As they say, when you do not apply pressure, you will feel it!
